"Where Chinese precision meets Japanese engineering excellence."

Shandong Xuri Graphite New Materials Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2013 in the Linyi County Economic Development Zone, Dezhou City, Shandong Province, China. With a total investment of approximately ¥466.5 million and a site covering 307 acres, we are one of Asia's most advanced graphite electrode manufacturers.

In 2019, we entered a joint venture with Sojitz Ject Corporation, a subsidiary of the renowned Japanese Sojitz Group — making us a Sino-Japanese enterprise combining best-in-class Chinese manufacturing with Japanese quality systems and global distribution expertise.

Applications

Industries We Serve

Carbon and graphite products are fundamental to some of Europe's most important industrial sectors.

01
Metallurgy & Steel
EAF steelmaking is the backbone of European green steel. Our UHP electrodes are engineered for continuous high-load operation in electric arc furnaces.
02
Chemical Industry
Graphite electrodes and special carbon materials serve critical roles in chlor-alkali production, industrial electrolysis, and chemical processing.
03
Aerospace & Defence
High-purity carbon materials for aerospace component manufacturing, heat shields, and specialist structural composites demanding precise tolerances.
04
Energy & Nuclear
Graphite's unique thermal and electrical properties make it indispensable in nuclear reactor moderators and advanced energy storage systems.
05
Battery & EVs
Artificial graphite powder for lithium-ion battery anodes, serving Europe's rapidly growing electric vehicle and stationary energy storage markets.
06
Non-Ferrous Metals
Electrodes for aluminium, copper, silicon, and ferroalloy smelting operations — precision-matched to the specific current densities of each furnace type.
07
Semiconductors
Ultra-pure graphite components for semiconductor crystal growing, epitaxial coating equipment, and high-temperature diffusion furnace applications.
08
Green Industry
Supporting Europe's decarbonisation goals: our products enable green steel production and next-generation clean energy technologies with lower carbon footprints.
