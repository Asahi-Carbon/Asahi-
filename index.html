"Where Chinese precision meets Japanese engineering excellence."

Shandong Asahi Carbon Graphite New Material Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2013 in the Linyi County Economic Development Zone, Dezhou City, Shandong Province, China. With a total investment of approximately ¥466.5 million and a site covering 307 acres, we are one of Asia's most advanced graphite electrode manufacturers.

In 2019, we entered a joint venture with Sojitz Ject Corporation, a subsidiary of the renowned Japanese Sojitz Group — making us a Sino-Japanese enterprise combining best-in-class Chinese manufacturing with Japanese quality systems and global distribution expertise.

Strategy & Innovation

The carbon industry is an important new-energy raw-material sector in China, and carbon products are widely used in numerous fields such as metallurgy, chemical engineering, aviation, aerospace, and nuclear energy. Considering the company's long-term development, ASAHI Technology has adopted a strategy driven by technological innovation to promote sustainable corporate growth. The company has invested over 20 million yuan to introduce advanced laboratory equipment and establish an independent R&D center, dedicated to producing China's highest-quality ultra-high-power graphite electrodes. ASAHI Technology aims to catch up with and surpass the standards of developed countries such as Europe, the United States, and Japan, thereby building a truly national Chinese brand. At the same time, the company is actively engaged in the R&D of cutting-edge carbon products, including fullerene carbon, nano-carbon, and graphene. By fully leveraging its technological strengths, ASAHI Technology is creating broader market prospects and driving the overall development of China's carbon industry.

Artificial Graphite Powder

High-purity synthetic graphite powder for lithium-ion battery anodes, industrial lubricants, refractory components, and other specialty material applications.

Standard retail and small-batch packaging format. Powder is sealed in multi-ply kraft paper sacks of 25 kg net weight, stacked on a heat-treated wooden pallet and secured with PET strapping for ocean and inland transport.

Ingredients

Raw materials and feedstock for graphite production — the same inputs we use in our own electrode and powder manufacturing, available to industrial buyers worldwide.

Calcined Petroleum Coke

Anode-grade calcined petroleum coke (CPC) used as a primary carbon source in graphite electrode and anode paste production. Low sulphur, low metals, high fixed carbon — sourced and calcined to electrode-grade specifications.

Anode-grade calcined petroleum coke (CPC) used as a primary carbon source in graphite electrode and anode paste production. Calcined at 1,200–1,400 °C to drive off volatiles, deliver high fixed carbon and the porosity required for impregnation and graphitisation.

Fixed carbon: ≥ 98.5 % · Sulphur: ≤ 0.7 % · Ash: ≤ 0.5 % · Real density: ≥ 2.06 g/cm³

Available in standard granulometries (0–5 mm, 5–25 mm) and custom screen cuts on request. Ships in 1,000 kg bigbags or break-bulk on request.

Premium Needle Coke

Highly crystalline needle coke — the flagship feedstock for Ultra-High Power (UHP) graphite electrodes. Anisotropic, low CTE, low sulphur — engineered for electrodes running the most demanding electric arc furnace duties.

Highly crystalline needle coke is the flagship feedstock for Ultra-High Power (UHP) graphite electrodes. Its anisotropic, lamellar structure gives finished electrodes the low thermal expansion and high electrical conductivity required by the most demanding electric arc furnace duties.

Fixed carbon: ≥ 99.0 % · Sulphur: ≤ 0.5 % · Ash: ≤ 0.2 % · CTE (a-axis): ≤ 1.0 × 10⁻⁶ /°C

Both petroleum-derived and coal-tar-derived needle coke available. Ships in 1,000 kg bigbags with full COA, MTR and origin documentation.
